Moving Arts East (MAE) is an arts and artist based organization established by professional dance practitioners living and working in north Suffolk. The aim is to offer dance focused opportunities for the community in the form of workshops, performances, collaborations, cross arts projects, training and the opportunity to prototype and present movement based work.

Moving Arts East is supported by The Cut, Halesworth, Suffolk and has helped to fund dance artists development training and performance work.
